About Us

CV M&G is located (headquartered) in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. We are a professional natural stone manufacturer for both raw material and carved creations. Founded in 1983, we offer blocks/slabs of natural stones (granite and marble), and carved stone creations. In addition to our presence in India, we also have offices in the following major hubs around the world: Seattle-Melbourne-London. With are continuous dedication and hard work we have created a global network of suppliers.

Our Process

Once you select a stone, we quarry it and bring it to our manufacturing unit for processing. We can supply the blocks as it is, if needed. Or, if slabs of stone is needed, upon confirmation of measures and thickness, we cut the block as per requirements. After cutting, the slabs go through rigorous cyclical-chain of washing, shining, and epoxy resin. In the end, each slab goes through test for quality. Only the highest quality slab makes it to the customer.
